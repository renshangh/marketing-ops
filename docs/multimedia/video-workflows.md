# Video Workflows

## Goal
Support short-form video planning and review.

## Standard flow
1. define hook
2. draft scene beats
3. identify caption style
4. produce clip externally
5. extract frames for review
6. revise pacing and clarity

## Best uses
- hook-first shorts
- educational snippets
- product demos
- case study clips

## Rules
- start with tension or conflict
- remove dead air
- keep captions legible on mobile
- review first 5 to 15 seconds carefully
