# Video Clip Template

## Purpose
Provide the standard motion-clip recipe for OpenClaw multimedia assets.

## Standard duration
- target duration: 12 seconds
- allowed range: 10 to 15 seconds

## Motion style
- subtle zoom or slow pan
- minimal movement
- no rushed cuts
- keep the image readable the whole time

## Source
- use a single image, diagram, or still frame as the base

## Output
- MP4
- social-ready aspect ratio
- clean export with no extra overlays unless requested

## QC
- verify the middle frame
- check crop safety
- check motion smoothness
- confirm the final duration is within range
